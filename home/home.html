About

This webiste is about two sports commonly played by women: basketball and netball. The Basketball and Netball pages share a little history about each of the sports. The Players page has images of famous players in both netball and basketball. On the Map page, viewers can discover places in the world where basketball and netball are commonly played. Additionally, there is a quiz under the Game page that viewers can interact width. We hope you have fun exploring the site!
